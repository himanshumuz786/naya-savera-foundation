Empowering Lives, Inspiring Change

Naya Savera Foundation is a social initiative based in Pune, Maharashtra, working towards education, community welfare, youth development, environmental sustainability, and social upliftment. We believe every individual deserves equal opportunities to grow and thrive.

Join Our Mission

About Us

Naya Savera Foundation was established with a vision to create a brighter future through compassion, community engagement, and meaningful action. We aim to bring positive change by supporting individuals and communities through awareness, education, and collaborative initiatives.

🌟 Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where every individual has access to opportunities, resources, and support needed for a dignified and fulfilling life.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower communities through education, health awareness, environmental initiatives, youth development, and social welfare programs that create sustainable impact.

Our Focus Areas

📚 Education & Skill Development

Promoting learning opportunities, career guidance, and skill-building programs for youth.

🌱 Environment & Sustainability

Tree plantation drives, environmental awareness campaigns, and sustainability initiatives.

👨‍👩‍👧 Community Welfare

Supporting social welfare activities that improve quality of life for underserved communities.

🩺 Health & Awareness

Health awareness campaigns and community engagement programs focused on well-being.

👩 Women & Youth Empowerment

Encouraging leadership, confidence, and growth opportunities for women and young people.

🤝 Volunteer Programs

Building a network of volunteers who want to contribute towards meaningful social change.
